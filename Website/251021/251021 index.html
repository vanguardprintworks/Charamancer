Welcome to Charamancer!

IMG

Charamancer is a modular system creating custom miniatures for table-top role playing games such as dungeons and dragons. You the head, armour and weapons as individual parts then assemble them the pose you want, with the universal interfaces guaranteeing it all fits together.

The system can be used to build a classic party of adventurers, then represent them as they advance through epic quests, upgrading their equipment with new exciting loot.

But Dungeon Masters aren't forgotten, with parts available to create custom humanoid monsters using all the same options for weapons and armour.

Iconic characters from your favourite games, movies, and television are available, perfect to ac as companions and bosses in your games. And of course the modular parts means once they're defeated their equipment can be weilded by the victorious heroes!

How it works

If you're fortunate enough to own a 3D printer you can buy the digital files for parts, allowing you to print them as many times as you like, making upgrading characters and building mobs of enemies a breeze. Find all the parts on the official Cults3D store.

If you want a physical model of a character you can purchase it through the Etsy store. You'll need to complete a form detailing which posts you want and this website will help you choose. All the options are down below

If you need little inspiration check out some completed designs.
