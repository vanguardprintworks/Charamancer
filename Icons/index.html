Charamancer

Charamancer is a modular system creating custom miniatures for tabletop role-playing games such as Dungeons & Dragons. You can select the head, armour, and weapons as individual parts, then assemble them in the pose you want, with universal interfaces guaranteeing everything fits together.

The system can be used to build a classic party of adventurers, then represent them as they advance through epic quests, upgrading their equipment with new exciting loot.

Dungeon Masters aren't forgotten, with parts available to create custom humanoid monsters using all the same options for weapons and armour.

Iconic characters from your favourite games, movies, and television are available, perfect to act as companions and bosses in your games. And of course, the modular parts mean once they're defeated their equipment can be wielded by the victorious heroes!

How it works

If you own a 3D printer, you can buy the digital files for parts, allowing you to print them as many times as you like, making upgrading characters and building mobs of enemies a breeze. Find all the parts on the official Cults3D store.

Cults3D store

If you want a physical model of a character you can purchase it through the Etsy store. You'll need to complete a form detailing which parts you want and this website will help you choose. All the options are down below.
